**Wolves of Wand Street — Super Quickplay Edition**

**Simultaneous Blitz**

*Players: 3–4   |   Rounds: 3   |   Turn: \~30 sec   |   Game: \~10 min*

# **Game Setup** *(This is automated in playingcards.io, see Quickplay)*

## **Player Setup**

* Each player starts with 5 Investors. These act like troops for attacking and defending Lands.

* Each player starts with 2 lands placed face-up in front of them: 1 \[***Thriving City***\] and 1 \[***Hamlet***\].

* Place 2 Investors in each starting land. The remaining 1 Investor is placed in your hand.

* Each player has their own Draw pile of 12 Draw cards.

* Each player starts with 3 Gold and 2 cards drawn from their Draw pile. 

## **Market and Land Pool Setup**

* The Land Pool (Title Deed) sits face-up in the centre with the remaining lands:

      4 \[***Hamlet***\] and 1 \[***The King’s Castle***\].

* The Market displays 4 face-up cards from the Market pile.

## **Round Format**

A round is played in three phases: the **INCOME** and **ACTION** phases are done simultaneously by all players; the **ATTACK & CONTEST** phase is done sequentially, with turn order determined randomly using dice (or Turn Chips in PCIO)

| INCOME All Players | Gain 3 Gold. Gain Investors from your lands into your hands.     \[*Hamlet*\]  \= 1, \[*Thriving City*\] \= 2, \[*The King’s Castle*\] \= 3  Draw 2 cards from your Draw pile.  *(Some of this is automated in playingcards.io, see Quickplay)* |
| :---- | :---- |
| **ACTION All Players** | All players may: Buy market cards using Gold *If two or more players want the same market card, the player with the earliest turn order buys it.* Play any card not marked \[***Attack***\] Place Investors from your hand onto lands you own. They **cannot** return to your hand except via card effects. Move deployed investors between your own lands.  Place \[***Attack***\] cards face down on the board, to be activated immediately in the **ATTACK & CONTEST** phase You may discard 2 cards from your hand to draw 1 card from your draw pile. |
| **ATTACK & CONTEST In Turns** | According to the turn order decided by dice or the Turn Chips (1 is first, 4 is last): Immediately flip face up \[***Attack***\] cards and resolve their effects Contest up to 1 land using Investors from your hand (see Contesting).  *If you play \[**Rapid Expansion**\]*, *contest up to 2 lands instead.* Claim a land from the land Pool (see Claiming) |
| **DISCARD** | If you hold more than 5 cards, discard the excess. |

# **Contesting (Attacking) Lands**

To contest a land, you must have at least 1 Investor in your hand.

Target any land on any player's board. Commit Investors from your hand.

* Higher Investor count wins. Defenders win ties and keep their land.  
* Afterwards, both Attacker and Defender lose Investors at a 1:1 ratio, placed back into their respective Investor piles (**not** the hand).   
* Undefended land (0 Investors): automatic capture, place at least 1 Investor on it.  
* Winning Investors become defenders of the captured land and cannot contest this turn again.

After winning, you may place additional Investors from your hand onto the captured land.  
Land modifier cards transfer with the captured land.

Example contests:  
**7** Attackers vs **4** Defenders:

* **Attacker wins**, both sides lose 4 Investors, Land goes to the Attacker  
* The remaining 3 Attacker Investors now defend the contested land.  
  **3** Attackers vs **3** Defenders:  
* **Defender wins**, both sides lose 3 Investors, Land stays with the Defender  
* The contested land is now undefended.  
  **3** Attackers vs **5** Defenders:  
* **Defender wins**, both sides lose 3 Investors, Land stays with the Defender  
* The contested land is now left with 2 Investors defending it.

# **Claiming Lands from the Land Pool**

Lands in the Land Pool are free to claim.

* To claim a land, place 1 Investor from your hand onto it. The land is now yours.  
* You collect income from claimed lands starting on your next INCOME phase.

# **Buying from The Market**

Market cards are bought using Gold, as denoted on the card.

* Bought market cards go directly into your hand. They cannot be played on the turn you buy them; they enter play when you next draw them into your hand.  
* Played market cards are discarded permanently (out of play).  
* After buying a card, fill the empty market slot with another face-up card.

# **Land Modifier Cards**

# **Minor Export, Branch Office**: Place the card face-up on a target land. 

* The modifier stays on the land permanently.   
* Modifiers stack with each other.   
* If the land is captured, the modifier transfers with it.

**You are ready to start the game\!**

# **Quickplay:**

Open PCIO, take a Seat, and when everyone is ready, start Round 1 by clicking   
“**\[Set up Round 1\]**”.   
This will automatically:

* Give all players 3 Gold,   
* Draw 2 cards from the draw pile,   
* Distribute 1 \[***Thriving City***\] and 1 \[***Hamlet***\],   
* Place 2 Investors in each starting land, and  
* Place 1 Investor into the hand.


Once a round is finished (after the **ATTACK & CONTEST phase**), click “**\[Start Next Round\]**”.   
This will automatically:

* Give all players 3 Gold, and  
* Draw 2 cards from the draw pile 

Due to engine limitations, please resolve Investors and Land Modifiers manually.

# **Scoring (End of Round 3\)**

**Final Score \= Land Points \+ (Gold ÷ 5\)**

* **Land Points:**  \[***Hamlet***\]  \= 1, \[***Thriving City***\] \= 2, \[***The King’s Castle***\] \= 3 

* **Gold:** Divide your remaining Gold by 5, rounded down.

* **Tiebreakers:** most lands, then most Investors on the map, then most Gold.

# If your Draw pile is empty when you need to draw, those draws are skipped. The pile is not refreshed.
