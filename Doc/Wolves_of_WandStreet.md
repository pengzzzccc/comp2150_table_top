# 🐺 Wolves of Wand Street

> **Players:** 4 &nbsp;|&nbsp; **Rounds:** 5 &nbsp;|&nbsp; **Turn:** ~45 sec &nbsp;|&nbsp; **Game:** ~15 min

---

## 1 · Setup

### 1.1 Map

- **13 territories:** 4 clusters (NW / NE / SW / SE) of 3 territories each + 1 central **Hub**.
- Each cluster contains:
  - 1 **Inner** territory → produces **3 Gold** per round
  - 2 **Outer** territories → each produces **2 Gold** per round
- The **Hub** produces **4 Gold + 1 Influence** for its owner.

### 1.2 Turn Order

- Shuffle turn order **at the start of every round**.

### 1.3 Neutral Investor

- 1 Neutral Investor is placed on the central Hub, hostile to all players.

### 1.4 Starting Territories (Simultaneous Choice)

- Each player **always** starts with their cluster's **Inner** territory.
- Each player picks **ONE** of their two Outer territories. (Can be done secretly and revealed simultaneously.)
- Place **2 Investors** on each starting territory.

### 1.5 Starting Resources

| Resource | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Gold | 4 |
| Influence | 1 |
| Investors (reserve) | 1 |
| Cards (from draw pile) | 1 |

---

## 2 · Resources

> Resources are gained **at the start of each round** — i.e. after every player has taken a turn, everyone simultaneously gains Gold, Influence, and draws a card.

### 2.1 Gold

- **Earned** each round from your territories.
- **Spent on:** Market cards, Gold bids in contests.

| Territory Type | Gold per Round |
|----------------|---------------|
| Outer | 1 |
| Inner | 2 |
| Hub | 3 |

### 2.2 Influence

- **Earned** each round: `Base 2 + Tier Bonus + 1 (if you own the Hub)`

| Territory Count | Tier Bonus |
|-----------------|------------|
| 1–3 | +1 |
| 4–6 | +2 |
| 7+ | +3 |

- **Spent on:** Buying Investors (1 Influence = 1 Investor), some market cards.

### 2.3 Investors

- Can be **placed on your territories** or kept in your **reserve**.
- Can be **moved** to an adjacent territory (unless used to contest this round).
- **Buy:** Pay 1 Influence → 1 Investor added to reserve. Deploy onto any owned territory as an action.
- **Combat power:** Each Investor = **3 points** in a contest.

---

## 3 · Turn Structure

### 3.1 COLLECT

- Receive Gold and Influence income from territories.

### 3.2 ACT (Up to 2 Actions — may repeat)

| Action | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Buy Investors | Pay Influence, add Investors to your reserve |
| Contest a Territory | Attack an adjacent territory (see §4) |
| Buy a Market Card | Purchase from the face-up Market |
| Retrieve Investors | Move Investors from a territory back to your reserve |

### 3.3 FREE ACTIONS (Unlimited)

| Free Action | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| Deploy Investors | Place Investors from reserve onto a territory you own |
| Move Investors | Relocate Investors to an adjacent territory |
| Play a Card | Use a card from your hand |

### 3.4 DISCARD

- If you hold **more than 4 cards**, discard the excess **face-up** into the shared pool.

---

## 4 · Contesting Territories

1. **Target** any territory adjacent to one you own.
2. **Commit** Investors from your adjacent territories; spend **1 Gold per Investor** committed.
3. **Defender** may reinforce by deploying additional Investors from their reserve.
4. **Resolution:** Higher power wins. **Attacker wins ties.**
5. **Undefended** (0 Investors) → automatic capture, **no Gold cost**.
6. **Casualties:** Both sides keep **half** of their committed Investors (rounded down).

---

## 5 · Scoring (End of Round 5)

| Component | Calculation |
|-----------|-------------|
| Territory Value | Sum of Gold output of all territories you own |
| Gold | Total Gold ÷ 5 (round down) |
| Influence | 1 point per Influence |
| Cards | +1 point per Market card in hand |

> **Final Score** = Territory Value + (Gold ÷ 5) + Influence + Cards in Hand

**Tiebreakers:** Most territories → Most Gold → Most Influence.

---

## 6 · Cards

- Two separate piles: **Draw Pile** (ranks 2–6, free) and **Market Pile** (ranks 7–A, costs resources).
- **5 Market cards** are always face-up. Replenish after each purchase.
- Market cards stay in hand until played.
- Each Market card in hand at game end = **+1 point**.
